Just like when Im out in nature, I have never felt that time spent reading a book is wasted time. There is one problem though: I tend to forget parts of the books I read. Therefor I will do a short writeup about the books I read on my webpage. This way I can go back to the key concepts and since I'm writing it down I will further press the information in my brain. I believe in learning by teaching and That way I will repeat what I've learnt and I can also go back and get the key concepts.
